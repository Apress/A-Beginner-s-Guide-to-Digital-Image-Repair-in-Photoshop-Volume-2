Location of ICC Profiles on Computer Abstract and Device Link:

C:\Windows\System32\spool\drivers\color

C:\Program Files\Common Files\Adobe\Color\Profiles

C:\Program Files (x86)\Common Files\Adobe\Color\Profiles


To fix the issue in Windows for Photoshop CC 2021: 

1.Copy all the ICC files from C:\Program Files (x86)\Common Files\Adobe\Color Profiles
There should be about 16 ICC files.

2. Then paste them into C:\Windows\System32\spool\drivers\color

I did it and now I find all the Presets for Abstract and Device Link where they must be in the dropdown menus.
